info Understanding the Metrics (All metrics calculated for 3-year period)

CAGR (3Y): 3-Year Compound Annual Growth Rate
AUM: Assets Under Management (in Crores)
Beta: Volatility vs market (1.0 = market avg, <1 = less volatile)
Alpha: Excess return over benchmark (positive = outperformance)
Sharpe (3Y): Risk-adjusted return (higher better, >1 is good)
Sortino (3Y): Downside risk-adjusted return (better than Sharpe)
Treynor (3Y): Return per unit of systematic risk (Beta)
Max Drawdown: Largest peak-to-trough decline (lower is better)
Std Dev (3Y): Volatility measure (lower = less risky)
